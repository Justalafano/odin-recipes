Marinated Grilled Steak

Delicious steak that's marinated and grilled to perfection.

    ingredients
  • steak
  • minced garlic
  • olive oil
  • onion powder
  • pepper
  • lemon powder
    Instructions
  1. mix minced garlic,olive oil, onion powder, lemon powder, and pepper in a bag
  2. put steaks in bag and marinate overnight
  3. put marinated steaks on grill and cook to preference
