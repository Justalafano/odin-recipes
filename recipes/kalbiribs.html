Kalbi Ribs

Crispy Fatty BBQ Ribs

Thin sliced ribs cut against the bone. These are extra fatty/chewy ribs and should be grilled extra crispy.

    Ingredients
  • Kalbi Style Ribs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onion
  • Mirin
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sugar
  • Kiwi
    Instructions
  1. Add ingredients together to form marinade
  2. Grate kiwi into marinade
  3. Marinate meat for at least 8 hours
  4. Grill meat until charred
  5. Enjoy your ribs
